**Description:**  

- Clients are offered two tariffs. To adjust the advertising budget, the commercial department wants to determine a profitable tariff.
Analysis and pre-processing of data on the use of telecom operator tariffs and services by clients was carried out.
Hypotheses about the difference in revenues of subscribers of different tariffs and differences in revenue for subscribers from Moscow and other regions.
 
**Result:**
- Profitable tariffs were found and optimal sets of services for users were recommended.
